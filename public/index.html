Climate Change

In this visualisation, we show the impact of our greenhouse Gas Emissions on global warming and how activism for biodiversity and reforestation can impact this development.

Reforestation

Using the Reforestation token, you can model our reforestation efforts to compensate greenhouse gas emissions and see the positive impact on global warming.

Emission

Using the Emission token, you can choose the development Scenario of our Emissions until 2050. These scenarios impact the global warming shown in the heatmap.

Navigation

Using the Navigation token, you can zoom in on particular areas, tilt and rotate the map and change the perspective.
